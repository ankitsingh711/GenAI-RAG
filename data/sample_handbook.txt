ACME Cloud — Customer Handbook

ABOUT ACME CLOUD
ACME Cloud is a managed hosting platform for web applications. We handle servers,
scaling, and backups so engineering teams can focus on shipping product. Founded in
2019, we serve over 4,000 companies.

PLANS AND PRICING
We offer three plans. The Starter plan is $29 per month and includes 2 instances,
10 GB of storage, and community support. The Growth plan is $99 per month and includes
10 instances, 100 GB of storage, and email support with a 24-hour response time. The
Enterprise plan has custom pricing and includes unlimited instances, dedicated support,
a 1-hour response SLA, and single sign-on.

BILLING
Customers are billed on the first day of each month. We accept credit cards and, for
Enterprise customers, bank transfer. If a payment fails, we retry it after three days.
Accounts more than 14 days overdue are suspended until payment is made. To request a
refund, contact billing@acme.example within 30 days of the charge.

RESETTING YOUR PASSWORD
If you forget your password, click the "Forgot password" link on the login page. You
will receive a reset email within five minutes. The reset link expires after one hour.
If the email does not arrive, check your spam folder or contact support.

BACKUPS AND DATA RETENTION
We take automatic daily backups of all customer databases and retain them for 30 days.
Growth and Enterprise customers can trigger on-demand backups from the dashboard.
Backups are stored encrypted in a separate region from your primary instances.

SCALING
The platform autoscales horizontally based on CPU usage. When average CPU across your
instances exceeds 70 percent for five minutes, a new instance is added automatically,
up to your plan's limit. Scaling down happens when CPU stays below 30 percent for
fifteen minutes.

SUPPORT CHANNELS
Starter customers use the community forum. Growth customers get email support at
support@acme.example. Enterprise customers get a dedicated Slack channel and a named
account manager. Our status page at status.acme.example shows real-time incidents.

CANCELLATION
You can cancel at any time from the billing settings page. Cancellation takes effect at
the end of the current billing period; we do not pro-rate partial months. Your data is
retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.
